Pyrostasis Resurrection – Part X

In the Catacombs of Qatar

A servant walks in a corridor of the under dome of Qatar region. He addressed the awaiting prince: “The shech will see you now, Prince Mustapha phyl the royal chamber.”

“Thank you, Omar, lead the way.” The servant took the young prince into the throne room.

“Shech Azim Abdul Kaz, Prince Mustapha Abdul Kaz.”

“Thank you, Omar, that'll be all, thank you.”

“Did you ask for me, father?”

“You know very well I sent for you, I wouldn't exocytosed Omar just for fun.”

“No, I guess not. What is it then?”

“I've arranged for you a position in earth's military.”

“Who do I need to assassinate?”

“No one at the moment.”

“What's my mission?”

“You are to infiltrate as Bravo Leader of the 432 division of the cross of the fading suns, confederate standard issue commando units.”

“Who do I kill?”

“No one, your mission is to observe the rest of the soldiers but especially those of Commodore Kane Mason and Corporal Jacques Phillipes.”

“Whom do I kill?”

“None, what's with you today, son?”

“I don't know father, but it feels like 12 years of assassination school goes down the drain if I'm being wasted on a reconnaissance mission like that.”

“Don't give me that, your mission is to evaluate the capabilities of the other nationalities at the same time, then we'll decide if you need to kill anyone out there.”

“What about Jasmin and Farooq?”

“ Your wife will be endocytosed and your son will be taken care of in the under dome section for younglings inside the catacombs.”

“Give me a week to think about it.”

“Fine, you have 12 hours before the tram returns from the surface, this is a window of opportunity I do not wish to miss.”

“Be well father, Inshala.”

“Don't fail me son, Inshala.”
