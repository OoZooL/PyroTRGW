Pyrostasis Resurrection – part XI

Le Château

“Misye Jacques Phillipes to the consul chamber” was heard in the speakers of the Château.

“Okay, Okay, what's the rush?” He finished drinking his glass of red wine while a servant was waiting to collect it nearby holding an empty tray. A few more sips, then he put out his Cuban cigar inside his empty glass.

“Misye Jacques Phillipes to the consul chamber, now!!!”

“It's the fifth time they call you, master Jacques.”

“ I know, Rober, I know.” He lift his glass as if to put it in Rober's tray, then he threw it on the nearby wall where it spread to smithereens. “Sorry, Rober, I missed.”

“One day, young master, one day...” Rober clinched his fist in the young master's general direction and went to activate the cleaning bots.

The young master sneered to himself and went to the consul chamber while a sixth calling overhauled him while entering the chamber. “Bonjour papa, what do you want from me today?”

“You little spoiled brat, that's the last time when you almost gave me a heart attack while trying to call on you.”

“Don't worry papa, there'll be more chances.”

“No, there won't. Luckily for me, I lost in a bet over a pool game to your espionage school head master, and you are to be sent away.”

“What? No way. Where to, papa?”

“The 432 division of the cross of the fading suns confederate special commando unit, bravo team.”

“What for, papa?”

“To spy on a certain individuals, namely Corporal Ophelia Summer, Seargent Mustapha Abdul, and Commodore Kane Mason.”

“But I'll die for sure, my dear papa.”

“Many times over what you deserve for your heinous and child like behaviour towards your friends in espionage school and the labour force of your family, not including several family members who preffered to sent you to Cyberia, to live in Gulag refugee camp...”

“But papa, let's negotiate, I'll give up my cells in Brussels and Rome.”

“Already captured.”

“What about Chile & Costa-Rica?”

“Surrounded already.”

“Even Malasia and Darpour?”

“Even, give it up son, all your cells have been exposed once I told your school head master you're considering to defect to the other side, not to mention where you parked his hover-craft.”

“As if he would believe you papa.”

“As if he didn't exocytosed eight agents for this task alone.”

“Okay, papa, just one more question if I may, is this Ophelia hot?”

“Infernally.”

“Do you mean yes?”

“Positive, son.” He said after a sigh of despair.

“So why didn't you say yes in the first place?”

“Stop acting so stupid, you're not in character yet.”

“Always two steps before you, papa, I was in character all the way.”

“So does it mean you'll do it?”

“Papa, you had me with 'Ophelia'.”

“Get out Jacques, I need to reconsider taking away my life.”

“Don't commit suicide, papa. What will I do without you?”

“You'll do everything exactly the same way as you ever did before, nothing really changes with you, son.”

“Farewell father.”

“Goodbye son and good luck.”

“See you later, papa.” Jacques left the room, back to the mazes of bureaucracy which lay on his table. He opened the first drawer of his desk with a biometric scanner. In the drawer there was an old revolver, with six bullets in its drum. He looked at the gun for a moment, then locked the drawer back, when the loaded revolver still lying inside safe and sound.
